Puzzle box

I created this five foot lock box for Insomnia LRP, it had a hidden chessboard on top and six small alcoves. The players of the lrp had to find codes that told them to play a chess game in a certain order, when pieces were put on the board in the right order it would trigger an RFID chip that would unlock one of the secret compartments and let them get a piece of another puzzle.

Army of Two mask

This mask was made to translate a customers custom mask from video game Army of Two to a wearable and safe airsoft version. The eyepieces were resolved with tough mesh that will stop a BB up to 500 feet per second painted white for a ghostly effect in the dark and gloomy airsoft game parks it was used for.

Magpie's Mask

This armour set was created for a post apocalyptic character called Magpie, black cloth with silver/white armour plates and a gold mask added to the look of the character. The gasmask was created from a japanese style oni mask.

Magpie's Shell

The armour had to look tough and practical, able to stop a bullet or mutant spear if need be. But also appear stylish and near-futuristic. It incorporates scavanged bike armour shoulders, to protect the character when crawling through the small dank tunnels they use to avoid radioactive areas, a chest mounted torch in the style of police body cams, a wooden handled kukri with bandage mended grip and a scavanged military pistol that indicates the character has a skill for foraging. The weathering was done with real rust effects where appropriate and a mix of paint techniques.

Magpie's Shell (rear)

The rear included more details, two tanks for his air filtration mask marked o2 and Combat Stimz to indicate the combat drug dependancie common with stalkers in the fictional world. It also needed a "techspine" which is a fictional technlology that the character uses to act as a giger counter, communication device and hack into surviving pre-apocalypitc technology. The quilted leather hood protects from bumps and acid rain.

Magpie's Pistol

In a world where ammunititon is the most expensive commodity automatic weaponry is almost unheard of. I made the decision to arm the character with a basic glock, they are hard wearing, siplistic and easy to maintain as far as pistols go. They are also quite common as sidearms with military and police world over, this means they would be more likely to survive.
